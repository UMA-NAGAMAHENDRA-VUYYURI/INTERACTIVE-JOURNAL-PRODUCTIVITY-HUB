Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When you upgrade, you'll get immediate access to the new features. Downgrades will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

Is there a free trial for paid plans?

Yes! All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. You won't be charged until the trial period ends, and you can cancel anytime during the trial without any charges.

How secure is my journal data?

We take security very seriously. All your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard AES-256 encryption and regular security audits to ensure your private thoughts stay private.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and in some regions, Apple Pay and Google Pay.
